# Legal Launch Checklist

These generated policies are templates, not legal advice.

- [ ] Adult Owner identity and public contact are accurate.
- [ ] Minimum age and first-visit confirmation are approved.
- [ ] Membership prices, renewal, cancellation, grace, refund, dispute, and tax language match provider settings.
- [ ] Privacy, cookies, first-party analytics/opt-out, retention, deletion, export, email, and advertising disclosures are reviewed.
- [ ] Virtual Currency Policy repeats that balances cannot be bought, transferred, redeemed, withdrawn, or exchanged.
- [ ] No page suggests real-money wagering, deposits, cash prizes, cash-out, investment, paid odds, or paid virtual currency.
- [ ] Advertising categories/consent and sponsor disclosures are approved.
- [ ] Proprietary copyright, third-party notices, and every asset license are correct.
- [ ] PayPal/Square/advertising acceptable-use policies and approvals are current.
- [ ] A qualified professional reviews areas appropriate to the owner, location, audience, taxes, subscriptions, privacy, and commercial licensing.

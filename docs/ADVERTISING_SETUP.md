# Advertising Setup

Ads are disabled by default and the Adult Owner owns the publisher account and payouts. Develop with clearly labeled placeholders. Apply to the selected provider with a truthful play-money site description, wait for approval, configure provider-issued identifiers and `ads.txt`, review consent/privacy duties, block inappropriate categories, and enable only after Adult Owner approval.

Ad-free entitlements are enforced server-side. Ads never appear over controls, in unresolved rounds or payment flows, beside confirmation buttons, or on authentication pages. Approval and earnings are not guaranteed. See Part S of [SETUP_EVERYTHING.md](SETUP_EVERYTHING.md).
